About Rob Birch

Rob got into programming when he was twenty. His biggest success in programming was when he wrote a program that reduced the amount of variance in a mortgage portfoliio's escrow. As a result, his company was ranked as one of the best servicers because they had fewer compliants. His focus is on VBA, VBS, JavaScript, Python, React, Redux, Node.js, Express, MongoDB, Mocha, Chai, and Jest. His resume can be viewed here.

When Rob isn't programming he is reading books, running, board gaming, and cooking good food with his wife in the Bay Area. He has lived in California, Colorado, Texas and South Korea. Occasionally, he'll tell 1 liners like, "There are 10 types of people in this world. Those that know binary, and those that don't". He isn't sure which 1 he is though...
