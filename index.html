About Rob Birch

Rob got into programming when he discovered how much he liked it and how much value it adds to people's lives. His first real foray into creating a program that created a lot of value was when he wrote a program that helped reduced the amount of escrow analyses in a mortgage portfolio. Customers were happy that their payments were more stable throughout the year and as a consquence his company was ranked as one of the best mortgage servicer of the year. His focus is on JavaScript, React, Redux, Node.js, Express, MongoDB, Mocha, Chai, and Jest.

When Rob isn't programming he is reading books on finance, economics and programming, running and cooking good food with his wife in the Bay Area. Occasionally , he'll tell one liners like, "There are ten types of people in this world. Those that know binary, and those that don't". He isn't sure which one he is though...
